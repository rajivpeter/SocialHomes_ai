Overview

Built for Social Housing. Powered by AI.

SocialHomes.Ai is a comprehensive housing management platform designed exclusively for UK social housing providers. It replaces fragmented legacy systems with a single, intelligent operating system that covers the full spectrum of housing operations.

🏗 Property Management

Full property lifecycle with compliance tracking, EPC ratings, damp risk scoring, interactive maps, and 3D building visualisation.

👥 Tenancy Management

Complete tenant records with rent balances, arrears tracking, Universal Credit status, vulnerability flags, and household composition.

🔧 Repairs & Maintenance

End-to-end repairs with priority triage, SLA tracking, contractor assignment, and work history across Emergency, Urgent, Routine, and Planned works.

🛡 Compliance & Safety

Big 6 compliance dashboard (Gas, Electrical, Fire, Asbestos, Legionella, Lifts) with Awaab's Law countdown timers and automated alerts.

£ Rent & Income

Rent collection dashboard with arrears analysis, AI-prioritised worklists, Universal Credit impact tracking, and payment method breakdowns.

💬 Complaints Handling

Housing Ombudsman-ready complaint management with stage tracking, TSM metrics, category breakdown, and resolution workflow.

🧠 AI Intelligence Centre

8 AI prediction models for arrears trajectory, damp risk, complaint escalation, weather impact, and proactive welfare alerts.

📊 30+ Reports

Regulatory returns (TSM, H-CLIC, RSH), operational performance, compliance reports, governance packs, and tenant-facing summaries.

HACT v3.5 Data Standard

The entire data model is built on the HACT UK Housing Data Standard v3.5, ensuring interoperability and regulatory compliance from the ground up.
